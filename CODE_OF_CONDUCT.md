# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Offensive comments or hate speech
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers through a GitHub issue or discussion.
